About & Contact

Built for SA Students.

By a South African student, for every student navigating university life.

ScholrZA exists because South African students deserve free, reliable tools — covering NSFAS, bursaries, universities, past papers, and the issues that actually affect their future.

Our Mission

We bring everything a South African student needs into one free app. No subscriptions, no paywalls on the tools that matter most.

No fluff. Just real, practical help for students navigating university life in South Africa.
