Trusted Student News. First.

Built by a South African student, for South African students.

VarsityNews exists because South African students deserve a news platform that speaks their language — covering NSFAS, bursaries, campus life, and the issues that actually affect their future.

Our Mission

We bring you the latest news on higher education, student funding, campus life, and graduate opportunities — all in one place, built specifically for South African varsity students.

No fluff. No paywalls on the stories that matter most. Just real, relevant news for students navigating university life in South Africa.
