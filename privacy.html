Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Varsity Survival Hub · South Africa

Varsity Survival Hub is built for South African students. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what we need to give you the best experience.

1. Who We Are

Varsity Survival Hub is a free student platform operated by Sudays Ibraahim, built for South African university and college students. Accessible at varsity-hub-alpha.vercel.app.

Questions? Contact us at sudaysibraahim14092009@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Varsity Survival Hub, we may collect:

  • Account information — your name and email when you sign in with Google or Facebook
  • Profile picture — your photo from your Google or Facebook account
  • Usage data — features you use, bookmarks you save
  • Device information — browser type, device type, general location (country/region)
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalise your experience
  • Save your preferences and bookmarks
  • Send you important account updates
  • Improve our platform and fix bugs

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Google & Facebook Sign-In

When you sign in using Google or Facebook, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) from those services. We only use this to create your account.

Your sign-in is handled securely via Supabase Auth. We never see or store your Google or Facebook password.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you signed in between visits
  • Remember your bookmarks and preferences
  • Measure site performance

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account at any time by emailing us.

7. Third-Party Services

Varsity Survival Hub uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • Google OAuth — sign-in
  • Facebook OAuth — sign-in
  • GNews / NewsData API — news articles
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Resend — email notifications
  • Groq / Anthropic — AI assistant

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sudaysibraahim14092009@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Varsity Survival Hub is intended for users aged 16 and older (university/college students). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our platform. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
