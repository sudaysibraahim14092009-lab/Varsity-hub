Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · VarsityNews, part of Varsity Survival Hub

VarsityNews is built for South African students. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what we need to give you the best experience.

1. Who We Are

VarsityNews is a student news platform operated by Varsity Survival Hub, built for South African university and college students. Our website is accessible at varsity-hub-alpha.vercel.app/news.html.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at sudaysibraahim14092009@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use VarsityNews, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign in with Google or Facebook
  • Profile picture — your profile photo from your Google or Facebook account
  • Usage data — articles you read, bookmarks you save, and categories you follow
  • Device information — browser type, device type, and general location (country/region)
  • Subscription data — if you subscribe, your payment is processed securely by our payment partner

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your VarsityNews account
  • Personalise your news feed and recommendations
  • Save your bookmarks and reading preferences
  • Send you important updates about your account or subscription
  • Improve our platform and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Google & Facebook Sign-In

When you sign in using Google or Facebook, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) from those services. We only use this to create your VarsityNews account.

Your sign-in is handled securely via Supabase Auth, a trusted authentication provider. We never see or store your Google or Facebook password.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you signed in between visits
  • Remember your bookmarks and preferences
  • Track your free article usage (2 free articles before paywall)
  • Measure site performance (analytics)

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing us.

7. Third-Party Services

VarsityNews uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • Google OAuth — sign-in
  • Facebook OAuth — sign-in
  • GNews API — news articles
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Resend — email notifications

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sudaysibraahim14092009@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

VarsityNews is intended for users aged 16 and older (university/college students). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our platform. Continued use of VarsityNews after changes means you accept the updated policy.
